WASTE MANAGEMENT

CURRENT SITUATION OF INDIA

The situation forced us to step ahead and work for our environment. The plastic pollution reducing ecosystems' ability to adapt to climate change, directly affecting millions of people's livelihoods, food production capabilities and social well-being.

The first step is to aware about types of dustbin that is mandatory to use in household work:

Wet waste includes bio degredable wastes eg.rotten eggs, fruits, vegetables and peels, coconut shells, tea bags, used tea powder, left-over food, garden waste

Dry waste includes bio degredable wastes eg. plastic, aluminium cans, newspapers, aerosol cans, lights, broken bulbs, glass bottles, plastic bottles, gift wrappers, cards, cardboards.

If there is a biscuit packet so the biscuits should be dropped into a green dustbin, and the biscuit wrapper is to be dropped in a blue dustbin
